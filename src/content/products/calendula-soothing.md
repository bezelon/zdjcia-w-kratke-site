Calendula Soothing Soap is formulated specifically for those with sensitive, dry, or irritated skin. Calendula is renowned for its skin-calming, anti-inflammatory, and healing properties. We have left this bar completely unscented to prevent any irritation from essential oils, making it safe for the whole family, including children.

### Key Benefits:

- **Sensitive Skin Friendly**: 100% free of artificial fragrances, essential oils, and synthetic colorants.
- **Calendula Petals**: Real petals gently massage the skin while infusing healing nutrients.
- **Moisture Rich**: Sweet almond oil adds extra vitamin E and deep hydration.

### How to Use:

Lather between hands with water, apply to face or body, then rinse thoroughly. Safe for daily use on extremely sensitive or dry skin types.
