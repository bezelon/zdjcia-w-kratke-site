Lavender Fields Soap is handcrafted in small batches to preserve its natural nourishing properties. Lavender oil is widely known for its soothing aroma, which helps to ease tension and promote a restful sleep. This bar produces a rich, creamy lather that gently cleanses without stripping the skin's natural moisture.

### Key Benefits:

- **Calming Aroma**: Pure lavender essential oil provides a natural aromatherapy experience.
- **Nourishing Formula**: Shea butter and olive oil moisturize and soften the skin.
- **Gentle Exfoliation**: Dried lavender buds offer a very mild, pleasant exfoliation.

### How to Use:

Lather between hands with water, apply generously to face and body, then rinse thoroughly. To extend the life of your soap, keep it dry between uses in a draining soap dish.
